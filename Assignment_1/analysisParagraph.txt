My plan was to figure out the frequency of the letters in the ciphertext,
create a dictionary that mapped the frequencies of the ciphertext to real
life frequencies. Then, do any manual subsitutions since it won't be perfect.

Sources:
For the real world frequencies:
http://pi.math.cornell.edu/~mec/2003-2004/cryptography/subs/frequencies.html

For finding out how to count the numbers of occurances of a letter in the ciphertext:
https://stackoverflow.com/questions/1155617/count-the-number-of-occurrences-of-a-character-in-a-string

For combining the ciphertext frequency list and the real frequency list into a dictionary:
https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/python-convert-two-lists-into-a-dictionary/
